WebGIS

The interactive WebGIS application is a powerful tool designed to help users explore and understand landslide risks. It allows users to interact with various data layers, switch between different views, and gain insights into how landslide risks and population exposure are distributed across a region. This tool is invaluable for stakeholders, researchers, and policymakers working in disaster risk management and urban planning. By providing a detailed and accessible way to visualize and analyze landslide data, it supports informed decision-making and helps protect communities from potential hazards.
